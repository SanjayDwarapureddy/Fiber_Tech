FRP doors

FRP doors are engineered from high-strength fiberglass and resin, offering exceptional durability and resistance to corrosion. Their lightweight nature makes them easy to install and ideal for both residential and commercial applications. With a variety of designs and finishes, FRP doors provide a versatile and long-lasting solution for any space.

WPC doors

Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) doors offer the perfect blend of wood aesthetics and modern durability. They are resistant to moisture, termites, and rot, making them ideal for any environment. With low maintenance requirements and a variety of design options, WPC doors provide both beauty and practicality for your spaces.

WPC door frames

Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) door frames offer superior durability and resistance to moisture, termites, and rot, making them an ideal choice for both interior and exterior applications. Additionally, WPC door frames are eco-friendly, made from recycled materials, and contribute to sustainable building practices.
